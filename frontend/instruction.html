üöÄ Getting Started

Welcome to EchoTrack SchoolCore!

EchoTrack SchoolCore is a comprehensive platform designed to help schools manage waste, track resources, and optimize unused spaces efficiently.

What You Can Do:

  • Track Daily Waste: Record daily waste collection with photos and categorization
  • Submit Weekly Reports: Comprehensive weekly waste management summaries
  • Create Monthly Reports: Detailed monthly analysis and performance tracking
  • Report Unused Rooms: Document and suggest uses for unused school spaces
  • Manage Resources: Report broken items, request new equipment
  • View Analytics: Track performance and waste management trends

System Requirements:

  • Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
  • Stable internet connection
  • Camera or phone for photo documentation
  • Valid school email address

üìä Dashboard Guide

Understanding Your Dashboard:

Quick Stats Section:

  • Total Entries: Number of submissions this month
  • Pending Submissions: Forms awaiting completion

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I save my form and complete it later?

A: Yes! Click "Save as Draft" button. Your progress will be saved and you can return to complete it anytime.

Q: What if I forget to submit my daily entry?

A: You can still submit it the next day, but it will be marked as late. Try to submit before 5:00 PM daily.

Q: How many photos should I upload?

A: For daily entries: 2-5 photos. For room reports: minimum 2 photos required. Maximum 10 photos per report.

Q: What photo format is accepted?

A: JPG, PNG, and WEBP formats. Maximum 5MB per photo.

Q: Can I edit my submission after submitting?

A: You can view your submissions but cannot edit them once submitted. Make sure to review before submitting.

Q: How do I reset my password?

A: Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page and follow the email instructions.

Q: Can I use my phone to submit entries?

A: Yes! The platform is fully mobile-responsive. You can use any device with a web browser.

Q: Who can see my submissions?

A: Your submissions are visible to your section heads, administrators, and the principal for review.

Q: What if I find a technical issue?

A: Contact technical support at [email protected] or call 0701078584

Q: Is my data secure?

A: Yes! All data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your information with third parties.
